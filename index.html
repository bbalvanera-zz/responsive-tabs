    Responsive Tabs Directive

    This simple AngularJS directive transforms an initial Tabs display into an Accordion display based on a given break point.

    Example

    display-mode={{display}} Heading 1 content Heading 2 content Heading 3 content Heading 4 content
    Resize your browser window to see the tabs change dynamically based on window size 
    <responsive-tabs justified="true" break-point="769"> ...
